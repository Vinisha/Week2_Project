# Account Setup Guide

## Creating an account

1. Download the shopping app from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Open the app and tap **Sign up**.
3. Enter your email address and create a password of at least 8 characters.
4. Verify your email by tapping the link we send you.

## Resetting your password

If you forget your password, tap **Forgot password?** on the login screen and
enter your email. We will send a reset link that is valid for 30 minutes.

## Enabling two-factor authentication

Go to **Settings > Security > Two-factor authentication** and follow the prompts.
You can use an authenticator app or receive codes by SMS. Two-factor
authentication is strongly recommended to keep your account secure.

## Deleting your account

To delete your account, go to **Settings > Account > Delete account**. This
action is permanent and removes your order history after 30 days.
