Shipping Information

Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 business days and is free on orders over $50.
Orders under $50 have a flat shipping fee of $4.99.

Express shipping takes 1 to 2 business days and costs $12.99.

We currently ship within the United States and Canada. International shipping is
not available at this time.

You can track your order at any time from the Orders tab in the app. A tracking
number is emailed to you as soon as your order ships.
