Returns and Refunds

You can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. To be
eligible for a return, the item must be unused and in the same condition that
you received it, and it must be in the original packaging.

To start a return, open the Orders tab in the app, select the order, and tap
"Return item". You will receive a prepaid shipping label by email.

Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 5 to 7 business days
after we receive the returned item. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the
return is due to our error (for example, a defective or wrong item).

Some items cannot be returned, including gift cards, downloadable software, and
perishable goods.
