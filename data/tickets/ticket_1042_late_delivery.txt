Support Ticket #1042

Subject: Order arrived later than the estimated date

Customer: My order was supposed to arrive in 3-5 business days with standard
shipping but it took 8 days.

Agent resolution: Apologized for the delay. Standard shipping estimates are 3 to
5 business days but can occasionally be delayed by the carrier during peak
periods. We do not refund standard shipping fees for carrier delays, but as a
goodwill gesture we issued a $5 store credit. If an order has not arrived within
10 business days, customers should contact support to open a lost-package claim.
